CODING (WITH ITS OWN COURSES)

Goal Oriented Academy (GOA) offers one of the most diverse and practical coding programs in Georgia, designed to take students from complete beginners to confident, job-ready developers through a structured, hands-on learning path. The academy covers a full spectrum of modern programming languages and technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, C#, Java, React, Node.js, and many more, ensuring that students gain exposure to both front-end and back-end development. GOA’s curriculum is built around real projects and practical challenges rather than memorizing theory; from the very beginning, students are encouraged to build websites, interactive applications, automated scripts, and even full software systems as their knowledge grows. Each course is divided into beginner, intermediate, and advanced segments, allowing students to progress at their own pace while steadily mastering more complex concepts like algorithms, data structures, database management, object-oriented programming, and software architecture. The academy places huge emphasis on creativity and problem-solving, guiding students through coding exercises that reflect real industry tasks, such as debugging, optimizing code, working with APIs, and collaborating on GitHub. What makes GOA stand out is its community-driven support system: students regularly help each other, participate in group projects, and receive direct mentorship from experienced programmers who provide feedback, code reviews, and personal guidance. GOA also introduces learners to essential developer tools, including VS Code, Git, Linux commands, and modern frameworks, giving them a professional workflow early on. Whether someone wants to become a web developer, game developer, software engineer, or simply learn coding as a valuable skill, GOA’s programs are designed to build confidence, technical skill, and real portfolio projects that demonstrate ability. By combining structured lessons, constant practice, teamwork, and motivational support, GOA’s coding education gives students the foundation and mindset needed to succeed in the world of modern technology.

MMA (Mixed-Martial-Arts)

Goal Oriented Academy (GOA) also offers a developing MMA (Mixed Martial Arts) program that combines physical training, discipline, and personal growth in a supportive environment. While GOA is primarily known for programming and personal development, its MMA branch focuses on giving students a strong athletic foundation and introducing them to the essential techniques of modern combat sports. The MMA training includes striking fundamentals from boxing and kickboxing, grappling basics inspired by wrestling, judo, and sambo, and introductory ground-fighting concepts similar to Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Instead of pushing students into high-pressure competition immediately, GOA’s approach centers on safety, structure, and gradual progression, ensuring that beginners learn proper movement, defense, stance, footwork, and controlled sparring at a pace that builds confidence rather than fear. The academy emphasizes physical conditioning as much as technique, helping students improve their strength, flexibility, stamina, and coordination through drills, bag work, partner exercises, and full-body workouts. One of GOA’s strengths is its encouraging atmosphere, where trainers guide students carefully and classmates support each other, making the training feel accessible even to those who have never practiced martial arts before. Beyond fighting skills, the program also teaches discipline, respect, focus, and emotional control — qualities that martial arts are known to develop. Students learn to push their limits safely, listen to coaches, maintain proper technique, and stay consistent with training. While GOA’s MMA program is not positioned as a hardcore professional fight camp, it provides a solid introduction to mixed martial arts, a structured pathway for improving fitness and self-defense skills, and a positive environment where young people can build confidence and physical strength. For many students, it becomes a powerful addition to their overall personal development, combining physical training with the motivational culture GOA is known for.
