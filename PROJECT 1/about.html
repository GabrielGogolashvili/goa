WHO WE ARE

Goal Oriented Academy (GOA) is a modern educational institution based in Tbilisi, Georgia, designed to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and mindset required to succeed in the digital era. GOA is more than just a programming academy — it is a holistic learning environment that combines technical training, personal development, and practical experience, aiming to produce skilled, confident, and goal-driven individuals. At GOA, students don’t just learn — they develop a vision for their future. Through structured mentorship, hands-on projects, and guided goal-setting, the academy helps students transform their ambitions into actionable achievements.

GOA offers a variety of courses designed for beginners and advanced learners, including: Programming & Software Development, Web Development, Robotics & Electrical Engineering, IT Fundamentals, Creative & Analytical Thinking Skills, Which can all be found at Home page Our curriculum is practical, project-based, and constantly updated to match modern technologies. Students learn by building real projects and applying their knowledge from day one.

Our mission is simple: To help students become highly skilled, goal-oriented individuals who can succeed in the global tech industry. We focus on: High-quality, engaging education, Discipline and growth mindset, Personal mentorship and support, Real-world skills that lead to real opportunities, A positive and motivating community, We don’t just teach coding — we guide students to build confidence, develop critical thinking, and pursue meaningful careers.

GOA welcomes students, young professionals, and anyone eager to build technical and personal skills. Whether you are:

  • A beginner wanting to learn programming, robotics, or IT skills
  • A student aiming to develop a strong mindset and professional readiness
  • Someone seeking career growth through practical skills and mentorship
GOA provides the tools, guidance, and environment to help you succeed.
